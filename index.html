Who are we?

Department of Anaesthesia and Intensive Care, the Chinese University of Hong Kong

We are one of the largest and most academically productive anaesthesia departments in Hong Kong. We are affiliated with Prince of Wales Hospital, a tertiary acute trauma hospital under the Hospital Authority, that provides clinical services for a wide variety of operations.
